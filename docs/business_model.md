# Volt-Aura: Strategic Impact & Business Model

## 1. The "Womaniya" Impact
Volt-Aura addresses the **Gender Energy Gap**. By automating complex energy markets, it allows women to achieve financial independence through household resource optimization without needing a degree in electrical engineering.

## 2. Revenue Streams
- **Performance Fee:** A 2% "Success Fee" on every profitable trade executed by the agent.
- **B2B Licensing:** Licensing the "Arbitrage Brain" to battery OEMs (e.g., Tesla, Luminous).
- **Community Pooling:** Enabling rural cooperatives to manage "Community Micro-grids" collectively.

## 3. 2026 Energy Landscape
In the current Indian energy sector, decentralized solar is abundant but poorly managed. Volt-Aura turns "passive" solar homes into "active" micro-enterprises.
