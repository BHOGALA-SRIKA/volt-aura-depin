# Volt-Aura: Impact & Vision

## 1. The Problem (2026 Energy Landscape)
Decentralized solar is abundant, but electricity prices fluctuate by up to 400% during peak hours. Most households are "passive" consumers who cannot manually track these fluctuations.

## 2. Empowerment Through Technology
Volt-Aura turns the household—traditionally an expense—into a micro-enterprise. 
- **Financial Autonomy:** Provides a passive income stream for women by automating complex energy trading.
- **Ease of Use:** A "set-it-and-forget-it" AI agent that removes the technical barrier to entry for the DePIN economy.

## 3. Scalability
The "Arbitrage Brain" algorithm is designed to be licensed to Smart-Home OEMs or deployed by rural cooperatives (like SEWA) to manage community micro-grids.
